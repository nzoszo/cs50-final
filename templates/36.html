How To Play
Game Type: Take Turns

Outsmart your opponent and gain more points than them.

  • When a player picks a number, that number is added to the player's score and removed from the grid.
  • All of its factors (e.g. 2 and 3 for 6), however, will be added to the other player's score and also removed.
  • NOTE: Only factors available on the grid can be added.
  • The player with the higher score when all numbers are used wins!
